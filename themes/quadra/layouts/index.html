Watch This Space Improv is an English language theatre community based in Barcelona that - Follows the Fun!

We offer improvisation opportunities every week for groups or individuals to get out there on stage! We welcome anyone who wants to have a laugh in English to come and watch and/or participate in hilarious comedy games with us.

Our events are mostly in English, but our Thursday nights at Bar De Retro in Gracia, Barcelona are also open to Spanish, Catalan or Spanglish acts and entertainment.
